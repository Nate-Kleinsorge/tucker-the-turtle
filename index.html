Tucker the Number Turtle

This 3D-printed turtle toy is a fun, hands-on learning tool designed for a three-year-old to practice number recognition and matching skills. The large green turtle features a removable brown shell with cut-out numbers, and five colorful mini turtles labeled 1 through 5. Children align each small numbered turtle with the corresponding number opening in the shell, helping build early counting, problem-solving, and fine motor skills. The shell lifts off to reveal a storage compartment inside the turtle's body, making cleanup easy and keeping all pieces together. Its bright colors and smooth, rounded shapes make it engaging and safe for little hands.

Features
  • Number Matching Learning Activity - Large cut-out numbers (1-5) on the shell correspond to the numbered mini turtles for early math development.
  • Removable Storage Shell - The shell lifts off so the mini turtles can be stored neatly inside the main turtle body.
  • Durable 3D-Printed Design - Made from sturdy 3D-printed plastic with smooth edges and vibrant colors for long-lasting play.

How to Use

Step One

Remove the turtle's shell and take out the five mini turtles.

Step Two

Place the shell back onto the turtle's body.

Step Three

Help your child identify each number on the smaller turtles' shells.

Step Four

Encourage your child to match each mini turtle to the correct number opening on the shell.

Step Five

When finished, lift the shell and store all mini turtles inside for easy cleanup.
